How to Make Apple Pie

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Peel and slice about 8 cups of apples.
  3. Toss the apples with sugar and cinnamon.
  4. Place the dough into a 9-inch baking pan.
  5. Coat apples with corn starch and lemon juice.
  6. Place apples in pan inside dough.
  7. Place second sheet of dough over apples and pinch crust together.
  8. Cut slits in top of dough.
  9. Egg wash top of pie and sprinkle with sugar.
  10. Bake for 40-45 minutes.
